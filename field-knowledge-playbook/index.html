How it works

Not a dashboard. A reusable workbook system.

What problem it solves

Experienced technicians often carry practical knowledge that is hard to transfer consistently to newer team members.

How the system works

Customer-reported issue, technician observations, likely causes, approved verification, escalation, final resolution, lessons learned, and training update.

What is included

Start page, dashboard, discrepancy log, issue pathways, evidence prompts, escalation notes, and coaching focus areas.

Practical business value

The workbook supports repeatable onboarding, better documentation, and more consistent capture of lessons from completed work.

Clear project boundaries

This concept uses fictional service data and does not provide technical procedures or safety instructions.


Optional explanation: why document-based interaction?

This project stays close to the way teams already use checklists, logs, and training packets. The interaction is preview, expand, download, and use as a workbook.

Workflow steps

Simple learning loop.

01

Capture the issue

Record the reported problem, field observations, and evidence.

02

Connect the pattern

Link the issue to recurring discrepancy pathways and escalation rules.

03

Turn work into training

Convert the final resolution into coaching notes and onboarding material.
