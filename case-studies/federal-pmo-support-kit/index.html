Kit overview

Built to bring structure to federal program work.

Federal programs often involve many moving parts: deadlines, stakeholders, action items, risks, meetings, reporting requirements, and documentation. This portfolio example shows how Flowshua can help a project team bring structure to that work.

The kit includes

  • Project charter
  • 90-day milestone schedule
  • Action item tracker
  • Risk and issue log
  • Weekly status report template
  • Executive dashboard
  • Meeting notes template
  • Closeout checklist

Designed for

This example is designed for federal contractors, healthcare programs, defense support teams, and organizations that need practical PMO support without unnecessary complexity.
