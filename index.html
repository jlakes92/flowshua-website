About

Veteran-owned project coordination built for work that cannot drift.

Flowshua Projects is veteran-owned and built from a background in Navy aviation maintenance, where complex repair work depended on clear ownership, disciplined coordination, technical documentation, and follow-through.

Today, Flowshua applies that same project mindset to rebuilds, upgrades, rollouts, and other work with multiple vendors, deadlines, risks, and moving parts. The goal is straightforward: give owners and teams a clear view of scope, schedule, risks, and next steps without adding unnecessary complexity.
